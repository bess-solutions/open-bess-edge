## Overview

The BESSAI Partnership Program offers structured collaboration paths for hardware manufacturers, system integrators, utilities, research institutions, and cloud providers that want to build on or contribute to the BESSAI Edge Gateway ecosystem.

Partnerships are non-exclusive, free of cost, and governed by the Apache 2.0 license and BESSAI `GOVERNANCE.md`.

## Research & Academic Partners

For universities, national labs, and research centers:

**Requirements:**
- Published or upcoming paper citing BESSAI or using it as an experimental platform
- Willingness to share anonymized benchmark results back to the community

**Benefits:**
- Listed as Academic Partner on website and documentation

## Partner Code of Conduct

All partners agree to:
1. Comply with the Apache 2.0 license terms
2. Attribute BESSAI properly in products and publications
3. Not misrepresent the BESSAI Certified badge level
4. Participate constructively in community discussions
5. Report security vulnerabilities privately via `SECURITY.md`
